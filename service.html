Easy Payment System (EPS) provides a number of services that enable effortless financial transactions for every sector of our daily lives. With a promise to enable a user-friendly, all-in-one financial ecosystem, EPS comes with a low-cost, secure, simple and fast solution in Bangladesh.

Merchants can receive their payments easily using EPS Payment Gateway. Using the EPS mobile apps, users can pay their utility bills and recharge their mobiles in a fast & secure way. Easy Payment System's customer support is available 24/7 to serve all its users.
